2-minute Noodles

Description:

2-minute noodles can be cooked by allowing the dried noodles to simmer in boiling water until they soften

The noodles can then be extracted from the water and served with seasoning from the satchels

Ingredients:

  • 250mL water
  • 2-Minute Noodles package
  • Optional: cooked vegetables

Steps:

  1. Boil water
  2. Place the noodles into the water
  3. Allow the noodles to simmer and soften
  4. Extract the noodles using a sieve
  5. Add seasoning from the satchels in the package
  6. Add cooked vegetables if desired
