White Rice

Description:

Rice can be easily cooked by boiling or microwaving it in water

Ingredients:

  • 250grams of white rice
  • 500mL of water

Steps:

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Add the rice in microwavable container
  3. Add the water which should be roughly double the rice contents
  4. Microwave for 16 minutes or until all the water has dried
